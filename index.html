About Our Brand

Bohst began as a simple idea inside Medal Blocks: achievements shouldn't live in a drawer. What started as a way to display race medals grew into a broader design language, built on clean lines and archival grade materials.

Today, Bohst is the design identity behind that work, spanning sports memorabilia, medals, coins, currency, and the collections still to come.
