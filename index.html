About Our Brand

Bohst began as a simple idea inside Medal Blocks: achievements shouldn't live in a drawer. What started as a way to display race medals grew into a broader design language, built on clean lines, high grade acrylics, fine wood moulding, and architectural layouts.

Today, Bohst is the design identity behind that work, spanning sports milestones, rare coins, historic banknotes, and flat collections, with more on the way.

Our official digital storefront runs through medalblocks.com/bohst.
